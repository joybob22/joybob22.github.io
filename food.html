Food

Pretty much the greatest thing ever is food. There is nothing more satisfying than going to a nice restaurant and chowing down on your favorite foods!

  • Sushi

  • Sushi is my #1 favorite food! Every time I take a bite of sushi I feel like I am getting a taste from heaven above. If you havn't tried it then go and try it out for yourself! You won't regret it.

  • Pizza

  • Pizza is my #2 go to food. It has the perfect combination of ingredients on a circle of dough. Pizza is so good it is nearly impossible to find someone who says they don't like it.

  • Tacos

  • Tacos are so amazing! They have the perfect balance of meat, chip, and cheese to make my mouth water every time I even think about tacos!
